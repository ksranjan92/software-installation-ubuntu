Install PyCharm Community Edition on Ubuntu:
============================================

Step 1: Download the PyCharm Community Edition binary file

Url: 

https://www.jetbrains.com/pycharm/download/?section=linux

pycharm-community-2023.2.3.tar.gz


Step 2: Install the PyCharm Community Edition

Extract the PyCharm Community Edition binary file

ls

tar -xvzf pycharm-community-2023.2.3.tar.gz

ls

cd pycharm-community-2023.2.3/

ls

cd bin/

ls

./pycharm.sh


Step 3: Verify the PyCharm Community Edition version

Open the PyCharm Community Edition IDE

Click on the "Help" menu and then click on "About"
